Vehicle inspection

As a driver, it’s your responsibility to make sure your vehicle is safe on the roads. No matter what type of vehicle you have, whether it’s a car, motorcycle or van, your vehicle is required to go through an inspection every year. Vehicle inspection checks that your vehicle is safe and roadworthy and ensures it meets road safety and environmental standards.
